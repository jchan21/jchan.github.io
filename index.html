All about Chance the Rapper

Who is Chance the Rapper?

Chance the Rapper a.k.a Chancelor Jonathan Bennet is a hip-hop artist. He has made many singles as well as many hits with artists like Kanye West and Big Sean. He has also worked with various renowned producers throughout his career as well.

Top 3 Songs by Chance the Rapper

  2. No Problem ft. 2 Chainz and Lil Wayne
  3. Juice

All about Chance the Rapper Albums

Chance the Rapper has made many albums. Here are his three latest albums which go from oldest to most recent.

Album Title Album Picture Album Description
10 day This album was a free digtal download and had 14 songs. The inspiration for the album name was when Chance got banned from his high school for 10 days.
Acid Rap This album contained 13 songs and was Chance's first "diamond" album, meaning it got over 1,000,000 downloads. This is approximately 4 times more than his last ablum "10 day".
Coloring book This is Chance's most recent album and by far his most popular. It contains 14 songs including one of his biggest hits "No Problem".
